Pamela's Ultra Rich Brownies

  • Time: 35-40 mins
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Pamela's Chocolate Brownie Mix       1 bag (16 oz.)
Oil 1/2 cup
Large Egg 1
Sour Cream 1 cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°. Lightly grease pan or line with foil and lightly spray or grease foil (for easy removal).

Step 2: Slowly incorporate all ingredients with a spoon, batter will be thick. Do not over mix. Pour into pan, smoothing evenly to the edges.

Step 3: Bake for 26 to 30 minutes, or until toothpick inserted into center of brownies comes out with slight crumbs. Do not over bake.

Source: Pamela's Ultra Rich Brownies

Easy Rum Cake

  • Time: 1hr 10mins- 1hr 30 mins
  • Serves: 8-10
Ingredients Quantity
Coarsely Chopped Pecans 1 cup
Yellow Cake Mix 1 (15.25-ounce) box
Vanilla Instant Pudding Mix 1 (3.4-ounce) package
Eggs 4
Vegetable Oil 1/2 cup
Water 1/2 cup
White Rum 1/2 cup

                             Glaze

       Ingredients    Quantity
   Butter                        1/2 cup
   Sugar 1 cup
   Water 1/4 cup
   White Rum 1/2 cup

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 325°F. Grease and flour a Bundt pan with at least a 10-cup capacity. Sprinkle the pecans in the bottom of the pan.

Step 2: With a mixer, mix together the cake mix, pudding mix, eggs, vegetable oil, water, and rum. Continue mixing until well combined. Pour the batter over the pecans.

Step 3: Bake for 50 to 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the middle part of the cake comes out clean and the cake starts to pull away from the edges of the pan.

Step 4: Make the glaze by combining the butter, sugar, and water in a small saucepan. Bring to a boil then remove from the heat. Stir in the rum. Pour over the warm cake while it is still in the pan. Allow the cake to cool before removing from the pan.

Source: Southern Bite Cake Mix Rum Cake

Chocolate Cream Pie

  • Time: 4hrs
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Pie Crust, Baked 1 (9inch)
Egg Yolks, Beaten 3
Large Egg 1
White Sugar 1 1/2 cups
Cornstarch 3 tbsps
Coaco Powder, Unsweetend 1/2 cup
Salt 1/2 tsp
Milk 3 cups
Butter 1 tbsp
Vannila Extract 1 1/2 tsp
Frozen Whipped Topping, Thawed     1 cup

Step 1: In a large mixing bowl, cream together egg yolks and sugar. Mix in cornstarch, cocoa powder, and salt. Add milk and stir gently.

Step 2: Pour mixture into a large saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until boiling. Remove from heat. Stir in butter or margarine and vanilla extract. Cool slightly, then pour mixture into pastry shell. Chill before serving. Garnish with whipped topping.

Source: AllRecipes Chocolate Cream Pie
